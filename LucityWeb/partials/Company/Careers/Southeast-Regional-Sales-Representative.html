Southeast Regional Sales Representative

Lucity, Inc. seeks a talented individual to serve as the Southeast Regional Sales Representative. The Regional Sales Representative generates sales revenue in order to meet or exceed established sales goals in software products and related services. He/she develops business opportunities in the southeast region (states include: Georgia, Florida, Alabama, North & South Carolina), through his/her own efforts and those of the Lucity sales team. In addition, the representative would respond to all incoming sales leads generated from any source/campaign. The ideal applicant will thrive in a fast-paced environment, be self-motivated, and have previous experience in local government sales/service as well as a proven track record in IT, Engineering, and/or GIS industries.

The Regional Sales Representative will focus on finding new business opportunities with new accounts or within the existing Lucity clientele. They are responsible for selling product licenses from the Lucity suite of enterprise asset management software, as well as services related to those products including, but not limited to, configuration and training services.

The Regional Sales Representative is responsible for achieving assigned sales quotas and goals for prospects located within the Southeast region.

The Regional Sales Representative is expected to prospect and develop business, respond to RFPs, other incoming sales leads and develop proposals for presentation to prospects and existing clients. They will coordinate directly with the East Region Manager in the planning, forecasting, and management of the sales territory.

Responsibilities:

  • Manage and develop a sales pipeline and forecast business weekly, monthly and quarterly.
  • Prospecting for and closing software sales to local government focused agencies in the Southeast Region. This includes municipal agencies (cities and towns), service districts, and counties.
  • Developing and negotiating product sales or service contracts.
  • Scheduling and performing product demonstration (via web and in-person)
  • Following-up on leads generated from corporate websites, trade-shows, seminars, and collaborative marketing efforts.
  • Utilize Microsoft Dynamics CRM (or other CRM sales tools at Lucity) as a daily sales tool and reporting system to manage contacts, leads, all sales opportunities, and forecast.
  • Together with the Regional Manager, create and manage effective brand and territory business plans.
  • Periodically attending national, regional and local trade shows/conferences
  • Maintain and submit bi-weekly activity reports, reporting on sales activities as directed by Regional Manager.
  • Develop and nurture partner channels for value-add solutions and services
  • Maintaining a highknowledge level of the company's products and services.

Job requirements:

  • Bachelor's degree (engineering, geography, or other technical focused education is preferred)
  • Proven sales and/or service related experience in IT, engineering, GIS, or other information management solutions
  • Commercial acumen, industry credibility, and professional demeanor
  • Excellent and demonstrable people and communications skills.
  • Strong relationship skills and the ability to work through long sales cycles.
  • Organizational skills, time management skills and ability to work independently in order to effectively structure a day to allow for key sales related activities, including sales prospecting time.
  • A proven record as a Self-starter individual.
  • Willingness to travel up to 50% of the time.

Required Travel: 50%

Required Education: BS in Engineering, Geography, or other technical-focused education is preferred
