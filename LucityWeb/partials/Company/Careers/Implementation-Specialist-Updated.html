Implementation Specialist

Lucity is a software company headquartered in Overland Park, KS that develops Computerized Maintenance Management Software (CMMS) for public works agencies throughout the US. Currently 40+ strong, we're now accepting resumes for the position of Implementation Specialist. Employees in this role undertake every aspect of their software implementation projects and present themselves as the face of Lucity, so must establish a positive baseline client relationship. Responsibilities include:

  • Project management: Bring comprehensive project management services to our clients’ implementation efforts, ensuring successful and profitable implementation of Lucity software products, Scheduling/coordinating project efforts and activities with the Lucity implementation project client-side counterparts.
  • Technical Expertise: provide support for the entire Lucity product line including new products/functionality/platforms that evolve each release.
  • All project implementation activities including (but not limited to):
    • Coordination & Management of all project activities & resources, plus scheduling & timeline development
    • Business process review & analysis ensuring a best solution for each client
    • Project documentation whenever necessary
    • Training end users and administrators both on-site and remotely via web-meetings
    • Conversion & loading of existing client data
    • Design & coordination of software interface development

If you are a technical person who enjoys (and is good at) working with people, keeping up with technology, managing projects and traveling, this job is for you!

Desired Skills and Experience:

  • hands-on experience providing technical services for implementation of COTS products
  • hands-on experience providing management services for implementation projects that done for local governmental agencies, is preferred, but not required.
  • Exceptional presentation and training skills
  • Excellent interpersonal and communication skills
  • Strong problem solving and analytical skills
  • Ability to travel extensively
  • Proficiency with Microsoft Office (Word, PowerPoint, Excel, Access)
  • Experience with databases (specifically Microsoft Access, SQL Server and Oracle)
  • Experience with Crystal Reports
  • Experience with ESRI software is required (ArcGIS and ArcServer at a minimum)

Required Travel: 50-75%

Required Education: BS/BA degree
